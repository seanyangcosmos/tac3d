Confidence is layer‑scoped. Mechanistic clarity often rises first, but without genetic reinforcement and maturing clinical endpoints, progress remains local. Support accumulated inside one layer does not automatically propagate across the stack.

Order is the guardrail: SYC core for structure, decision layer for cross‑evidence arbitration, overlay for interpretation. Inverting this order manufactures confidence—averaging away the very asymmetries that should gate transfer.

Let uncertainty remain visible. Treat transfer as earned: close the human‑layer and clinical gates with endpoints that actually test the mechanistic pathway in the right population and timeframe.
