When confidence propagates without structure, decisions inflate.

Omega topology keeps confidence layer‑scoped. Mechanistic clarity can be high, yet without genetic reinforcement and maturing clinical endpoints, progress remains local. Evidence volume is not evidence balance.

Order is protective: SYC core for topology, decision layer for cross‑evidence arbitration, overlay for interpretation. Reverse that order and you manufacture certainty by averaging away the very asymmetries that should gate transfer.

Let uncertainty stay visible. Treat transfer as earned: close the human‑layer and clinical gates with endpoints that actually test the mechanistic pathway in the right population and timeframe. Until then, keep support where it belongs — inside its layer.

Practical move: fund work that climbs the stack (validated markers, well‑controlled trials, convergent endpoints) rather than echo chambers within a single layer. Separate hypothesis sharpness from translation readiness; advancement is stackwise and conditional.
