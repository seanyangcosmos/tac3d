How it works
Simple. Fast. Honest.

TAC-3D doesn't tell you what to decide. It tells you if your reasoning holds up — before it's too late to change course.

Capabilities
What TAC actually does

Not just AI opinions. Real data, real gaps, real verdicts.

🔍
Checks your facts

TAC-3D searches the web to verify your claims — market size, valuations, benchmarks. If your assumptions are off, TAC will find it.

⚡
Three questions that matter

Do your facts contradict each other? Are they pointing the same direction? Do they hold up when things change? TAC scores all three.

When TAC finds a gap, it asks you about it. Add context, address the weakness, and see if your score improves.
