Many teams mistake internal coherence for global readiness.

Omega topology keeps confidence layer-scoped. Mechanistic sharpness, absent genetic reinforcement and maturing clinical signal, remains a local improvement—not a transferable advancement.

Preserve the call order: SYC core for structure, decision layer for cross-evidence arbitration, overlay for interpretation. Narrative follows topology; it should not fabricate it.

Let uncertainty stay asymmetric. Do not average across layers to manufacture balance. Transfer is gated until human-layer closure and clinical maturity align with upstream plausibility.

Bottom line: separate hypothesis refinement from translation readiness. Progress is earned stackwise, not inferred sideways.
