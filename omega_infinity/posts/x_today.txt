Omega Infinity topology signal: mechanistic isolation pattern.

1/ The mechanistic layer tells a clean story. Pathway logic, target interaction, or intervention rationale all look internally coherent.

2/ But the rest of the stack does not yet carry that support. Genetic grounding is absent, weak, or non-confirmatory. Clinical evidence is missing or too thin to transfer confidence.

3/ This is where people overread biology. A compelling mechanism can feel like proof when it is really a constrained local signal.

4/ Good reasoning systems should preserve the mechanistic support without letting it impersonate cross-layer validation.

5/ Mechanistic isolation is not failure. It is a warning against premature translation.
